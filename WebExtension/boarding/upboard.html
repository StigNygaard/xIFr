xIFr has been updated!

xIFr, your EXIF-viewer, has been updated to version .

If you haven't already, I suggest you to take quick look at the introduction. You might learn a thing or two about xIFr that you didn't already know:

xIFr - Getting started

Also, make sure to make a visit to xIFr's Options Page where you can adjust some aspects of how xIFr looks and behaves. Look for the wheel in xIFr window to access the options. My personal favorite settings are "Dark Theme" and the "Snap right" popup positioning.

xIFr version 3 upgrades the "extension type" from Manifest version 2 to Manifest version 3. This is a technical thing that hopefully doesn't affect users in practice, but there might be a small risk that xIFr stops working on a few pages/images where earlier versions used to work. If you discover any issues, don't hesitate to contact me and I will see if it is something I can fix. There are various ways you can provide feedback.

I should probably also mention that I have made a few changes to the wording of the Privacy Policy. It is only to make it clearer and avoid misunderstandings. There's no changes to the policy as such.
